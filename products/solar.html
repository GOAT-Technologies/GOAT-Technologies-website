MPPT

GOAT MPPT is a solution that aims to elevate green energy across various industrial sectors. Using an advanced maximum power point tracking algorithm, our solution enhances maximum solar energy harvesting from solar panels. It has an input rating of upto 540W and can handle harsh environmental conditions. Our solar charge controller has accurate battery and health monitoring capabilities that can be tracked on a mobile app.
